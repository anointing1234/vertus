  • FAQ
  • What is the check-in and check-out time?
    Check-in begins at 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be arranged subject to availability.
  • What is your cancellation policy?
    Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a one-night fee. No-show charges apply.
  • Do you offer airport transfers?
    Yes, we provide private airport pickup and drop-off services for an additional fee. Advance booking is required.
  • Are pets allowed in the hotel?
    We welcome small pets (up to 10 kg) in designated rooms. A non-refundable pet fee applies. Please notify us at booking.
  • Do you have on-site dining options?
    Our restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner daily. We also offer in-room dining from 6 AM to 11 PM with a curated menu.
  • Is Wi-Fi complimentary?
    High-speed Wi-Fi is free throughout the property, including guest rooms and public areas.
  • What wellness facilities do you offer?
    Enjoy our spa center with massage therapy, sauna, and steam bath, plus a 24-hour fitness center equipped with the latest machines.
  • Is parking available on-site?
    Complimentary valet and self-parking are available for hotel guests. Space is limited; please reserve your spot in advance.
